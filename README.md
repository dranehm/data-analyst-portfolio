## 📁 Projects

### Project 1: Superstore Sales Analysis 📊
**Status**: ✅ Complete

A comprehensive analysis of retail superstore sales data to identify trends, top products, and seasonal patterns.

**Key Metrics**:
- 9,800 transactions analyzed
- Total sales: $2,261,537
- 3 product categories, 4 regions, 793 customers

**Tools Used**: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter

**Key Findings**:
- Technology category drives 36.6% of revenue
- West region leads with $710K in sales
- Peak sales in November (holiday season)
- Top customer: Sean Miller ($25K in sales)

**Deliverables**:
- ✅ Cleaned dataset (9,800 clean records)
- ✅ 7 professional visualizations
- ✅ Statistical analysis & insights
- 🔄 Interactive dashboard (Tableau/Power BI) - Coming Soon
